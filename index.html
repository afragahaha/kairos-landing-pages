How does Kairos actually work? Is it fully automated?

Kairos combines three specialized AI agents—each handling a domain of your work. It connects to your existing tools (Gmail, Calendar, Slack, CRM, etc.) and handles tasks autonomously based on rules and patterns it learns from you. For sensitive decisions, it checks with you first. Think of it as a brilliant EA who knows when to act and when to ask.

What's the $2,500 setup fee for?

The setup fee covers dedicated onboarding: tool integration, security audit, workflow mapping, priority calibration, and a 1-on-1 session with our team to configure Kairos to your exact operating style. It's the difference between a generic tool and a true executive partner. Most clients see ROI within the first week of operation.

Is my data secure? I deal with sensitive information.

Security is foundational, not an afterthought. AES-256 encryption at rest and in transit, SOC 2 Type II compliance, zero-knowledge architecture, and a strict zero-training policy—your data is never used to improve our models. We meet the same standards as top-tier financial institutions.

Gmail, Google Calendar, Outlook, Slack, Salesforce, HubSpot, Notion, Asana, Monday.com, LinkedIn, and 100+ more via native integrations and API connections. If you use it daily, Kairos almost certainly supports it. Setup takes under 30 minutes per tool.

No long-term contracts, ever. Cancel with 30 days notice. And if Kairos doesn't meet your expectations within the first 30 days, you'll receive a full refund of your monthly fee. We keep our clients because they want to stay, not because they're locked in.
